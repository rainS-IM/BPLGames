Algebraic Functions

1. Solve for $x$: $2x^2 + 5x - 3 = 0$

2. Simplify: $\frac{(x + 3)(x - 3)}{x^2 - 9}$

Calculus II: Derivatives

1. $\frac{d}{dx} [\sin(x)e^x] = \cos(x)e^x + \sin(x)e^x$

2. $\int (3x^2 + 2x + 1) dx = x^3 + x^2 + x + C$

3. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin(x)}{x} = 1$

Linear Algebra

1. Find Determinant of $\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix}$

2. Vector Dot Product: $A \cdot B = |A||B|\cos(\theta)$

3. Solve: $Ax = B$ using Row Reduction.

Trigonometry

1. $\sin^2\theta + \cos^2\theta = 1$

2. Law of Cosines: $c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos(C)$

3. Convert $270^\circ$ to Radians: $\frac{3\pi}{2}$
